About Me

Hello! I'm Alex and I'm from Frederick Maryland - a small town about an hour north of Washington DC. I graduated with an International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma from Urbana High School. I am now an undergraduate Computer Science student at UCLA with Junior standing with a planned graduation in 2026.

What sets me apart from my peers is that I am not afraid of venturing into new fields and learning new skills. Too often you will see students declining projects or opportunities because they've never learned these such skill before- in contrast I embark on projects with the goal of learn the skills. This mindset has been the key to opening many opportunities for me in both my personal life and at UCLA.
